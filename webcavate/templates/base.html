Dungeon generator

This is a tool for making quick maps for desktop or online role-playing game tools. Load up textures for the floor (eg. some nice tiles) and the walls (eg. stone), give a cutout of the floorplan, and this tool will make you a map with a bit of shading so your players can have a sense of depth.
